Best Breakfast and Brunch in Fort Myers!

At Mon Paris, we bring a taste of Europe right into the heart of Fort Myers – the aroma of freshly baked croissants, the delicacy of French crêpes, and the joy of breakfast you can enjoy any time of day.

We use only the freshest local ingredients, and every day we bake something new so there's always a reason to come back and discover more.

The warm atmosphere is completed by gentle melodies in the background, making you feel as if you've stepped into a cozy French café.

Our Team

We, Veronika and Jan, are the new proud owners of Mon Paris, and together we've combined our passion for baking and great food with our love for Fort Myers.

Veronika brings European tradition into our recipes as a baker and chef, while James, a remarkable chef with a lifetime of experience and former restaurant owner, turns every dish into a memorable experience.

Together in the kitchen, we create a menu where French inspiration meets the freshness of local ingredients. Jan keeps the café running smoothly and makes sure we always have the best local produce, while Brenda is the welcoming face of Mon Paris – greeting our guests, taking care of service with a smile, and preparing excellent coffee and refreshing house-made lemonades.

With each of us bringing our strengths, we make sure that every visit to Mon Paris is filled with quality, freshness, and a warm atmosphere that makes you want to return.
